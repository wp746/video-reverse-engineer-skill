# Backend Handover Protocol (image2 + seedance2.0)

Use this reference during **Stage 5 (Producer Breakdown)** and **Stage 8 (Seedance Prompt Compilation)** to prepare the final deliverable for the production backend.

---

## 1. The Handover Stance & Internalization Principle

- **Internalization (内部自反应机制)**: The entire 10-layer deconstruction and mapping process happens *internally* within the agent's reasoning. The final output delivered to the user must be a **single, clean, copy-pasteable `.md` file** that contains the completed assets, bilingual prompts, storyboards, and 15-second Seedance video segments. Do not clutter the main response with redundant second-by-second analytical logs unless asked.
- **The Delivery Stack**:
  - **image2** (Google Banana 2): Used by the user to generate static assets (characters, scenes, props) and individual **storyboard frames**.
  - **seedance2.0**: Used by the user to render the final video segments by utilizing its **All-Round Reference (全能参考)** capabilities, feeding it the assets and storyboard frames generated by image2.

---

## 2. image2 Bilingual Asset Handover Protocol

Every static asset (characters, scenes, props) and storyboard frame in the `.md` deliverable must include **Bilingual Prompts (双语提示词)**:
1. **English Prompt**: For maximum model generation fidelity in image2.
2. **Chinese Prompt**: For quick user review, tweaking, and local adaptation.

### Character Asset Specifications
Include:
- **Core Parameters**: Age, gender, facial anchors, clothing layers, and stable identifiers.
- **Bilingual image2 Prompts**:
  - `English Prompt`: A cinematic character reference sheet containing face closeup, 3 views, and 9-grid clothing details on a white background.
  - `Chinese Prompt`: 中文对照版提示词，包含面部特写、全身三视图和九宫格细节。

### Scene & Prop Specifications
Include:
- **Master Scene Grid**: Unified style and palette.
- **Key-Scene Multi-Angle**: Hero, side, and reverse angles with bilingual prompts.
- **Props**: standalone definitions for symbolic/recurring items with bilingual prompts.

### Storyboard Frame Specifications
For every narrative joint, design an individual storyboard frame for the user to generate via image2:
- **Storyboard ID** (e.g. `STORYBOARD_01`)
- **Visual Description**: Framing, action, camera angle, lighting, and scene environment.
- **Bilingual image2 Prompts**:
  - `English Prompt`: Dynamic shot framing, setting, action, composition.
  - `Chinese Prompt`: 中文对照景别、构图与动作画面提示词。

---

## 3. seedance2.0 15-Second Segment Protocol

Since **seedance2.0** has a strict **maximum duration ceiling of 15 seconds per generation**, the timeline and prompts must be mathematically partitioned.

### The 15-Second Budgeting Rules
1. **Strict 15s Partitioning**: Segment the timeline into blocks of at most 15 seconds (e.g., `0-15s`, `15-30s`, `30-45s`).
2. **Scene Spanning (跨段衔接)**: If a single dramatic scene or setting takes longer than 15 seconds to resolve, split it across subsequent 15s segments (e.g. `SEG01_0-15s` and `SEG02_15-30s`).
3. **Carry-Forward Continuity**: In spanned segments, explicitly carry forward the exact asset references (`@角色-001`, `@场景-001`) and the ending frame of the previous segment to maintain seamless visual stitching.
4. **Action Pacing**: Ensure the action density described fits comfortably within the 15-second budget.

### All-Round Reference (全能参考) Binding
Instruct seedance2.0 to reference the exact assets and storyboards generated by image2 to lock pixel-perfect consistency (characters, scenes, prop styles, and camera pacing):

```text
================================================================================
【分段编号】: SEG01_0-15s (15秒段落预算)
【关联资产】: @角色-001, @场景-001, @故事板-01
【全能参考指令】:
  - 角色参考 (Character Ref): @角色-001.png
  - 场景参考 (Scene Ref): @场景-001.png
  - 故事板参考 (Storyboard Ref): @故事板-01.png
【安全区规划】: 
  - 0-1.5s: Hold initial frame matching @故事板-01.png, stable micro-motion.
  - 13.5-15.0s: Slow down motion, lock camera to allow clean stitching.
【连续性锚点】: Light source direction, costume material, face identity.
【出片提示词 (Seedance 2.0 Feeding Prompt)】:
(Playbook-compliant, physical motion English prompt instructing the model to strictly reference the character sheet and storyboard image)
================================================================================
```

---

## 4. Front-to-Back Handover Consistency Scan

Before finalizing the `.md` package, perform an internal sanity check:
1. **Bilingual Completeness**: Ensure every single asset and storyboard has both English and Chinese prompts.
2. **Mathematically Sound Timeline**: Check that segment boundaries are strictly `<=15s` and cover the entire runtime continuously (e.g., `0-15s` $\rightarrow$ `15-30s` $\rightarrow$ `30-45s`).
3. **All-Round Reference Matching**: Verify that the Seedance prompts explicitly state which image2 assets and storyboards they are referencing.
